I’m currently a Ph.D. candidate in Materials Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), where I am a member of ADVANCED ENERGY MATERIALS AND INTELLIGENT BATTERIES INNOVATION TEAM. I have previously completed my Master's degree at this lab. My research focuses on anion chemistry in electrolytes of electrochemical energy storage devices and materials used in electrodes in batteries. In addition, my research was focused on porous carbon materials for supercapacitors during my undergraduate period. More broadly, my learning interests beyond the energy storage devices encompass cross-link technologies as tools for batteries (AI, advanced physical characterization techniques). Hope I can get more chances to learn more knowledge to help the energy balance between nature and human activities in the future.
